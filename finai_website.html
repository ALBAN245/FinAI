Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel at any time from your account settings. You keep access until the end of your billing period with no extra charges.
Is my banking data safe?
Absolutely. We use read-only Open Banking connections compliant with DSP2. We never store your login credentials. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and PayPal. University licenses can be paid by bank transfer (SEPA).
Is FinAI available in my country?
FinAI is currently available in France, Belgium and Croatia, with expansion to Germany, Spain, Italy and the Netherlands planned for Year 2.
